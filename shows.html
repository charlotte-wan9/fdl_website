OUR SHOWS

Foundland has been bringing Chinese Musical to campus and contributing to the theater community on campus since our foundation. We have been highly praised by our audience for our ability to provide an exceptional introduction to this art form and the high standard of our performances as a student organization. Our productions have consistently made a meaningful impact on our audience, and our goal is to continue doing this.


We aim hold a musical theater performance once a year, at the end of each Fall semester. We hope to reach out to those who share our enthusiasm for musical theatre, and invite you to join our community and contribute your unique talents.
